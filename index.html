N - VISAGE

Considering the current state of mess that surrounds our ecology and environment, experts have proposed quick implementation of eco-friendly steps across all dimensions of human work to prevent our planet from the ecological and environmental collapse. Drawing a form to this inspiration taken, Pukaar comes up with N-Visage, the core event of We Care-The Green Movement. It is a case study event that aims to study and review different environmental related problems of Raipur and gather fresh ideas that can be suitably adapted to ameliorate the ailing environment and bring everything under label green.

  • Team size : Maximum of 3 members

EBULLIENCE

Now turn your minds sharp to predict the tree based on few defining, yet appealing parameters. Be a part of the event "Tree Hunt" at Pukaar!

  • Team size : 3 Members
